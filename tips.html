Important Tips for Bed Patients

बेड पेशेंट के लिए महत्वपूर्ण टिप्स — सही देखभाल, जल्दी आराम और बेहतर जीवन.

1

Change Position Regularly · नियमित पोजीशन बदलें

Turn the patient every 2–3 hours to reduce the risk of bed sores (pressure ulcers) and improve blood circulation.

2

Maintain Special Hygiene · स्वच्छता का विशेष ध्यान रखें

Clean the patient daily, keep skin dry, and act quickly at any sign of infection on the back, neck or other areas.

3

Water & Nutritious Food · पर्याप्त पानी और पौष्टिक आहार दें

Ensure adequate hydration and provide fresh fruits, green vegetables and protein-rich meals for faster recovery.

4

Breathing Exercises · सांस लेने की एक्सरसाइज कराएँ

Deep-breathing routines strengthen the lungs and significantly reduce the risk of chest infections.

5

Light Exercises · हल्की फुल्की एक्सरसाइज कराएँ

With the doctor's guidance, do gentle hand-and-leg movements — this improves blood circulation and joint mobility.

6

Keep Bed & Clothes Clean · बिस्तर और कपड़े साफ रखें

Keep bed sheets, clothing and the room clean, dry and well-ventilated at all times.

7

Regular Doctor Contact · नियमित डॉक्टर से संपर्क रखें

Schedule regular check-ups, follow the doctor's advice and administer medicines exactly on time.

❤

Love & Care · प्यार और देखभाल

प्यार और देखभाल ही है सबसे बड़ी दवा — emotional support heals as much as any medicine.
