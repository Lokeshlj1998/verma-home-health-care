Care Guidance

Important Tips for Bed Patients

बेड पेशेंट के लिए महत्वपूर्ण टिप्स — right care, faster relief and a better life.

1

Change Position Regularly

Turn the patient every 2–3 hours to reduce the risk of bed sores.

2

Maintain Special Hygiene

Clean daily, keep skin dry, and act quickly at any sign of infection.

3

Water & Nutritious Food

Adequate hydration, fresh fruits, greens and protein-rich meals.

4

Breathing Exercises

Deep-breathing routines strengthen lungs and reduce infection risk.
